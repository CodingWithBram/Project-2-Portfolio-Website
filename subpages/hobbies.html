Reading Books

I love reading books, especially in the fantasy and science fiction genres. Specifically manga, manhwa, manhua and light novels. One of my favorite series is "Infinite Mage," which combines elements of magic, adventure, and intricate world-building. The story follows a young protagonist who accidentally overheard a mage talk about magic and he got so curious he jumped over a wall to talk to him. After that he learned magic himself. This story is amazing because it uses mathematical concepts to explain how magic works in its universe, making it a unique and intellectually stimulating read.

Going to the Gym

Working out is really important to me because it helps me stay healthy and feel good. I usually go to the gym about three times a week, where I focus on weight training. I enjoy lifting weights because it makes me feel stronger and more confident. Overall, going to the gym is a great way for me to relieve stress and maintain a balanced lifestyle.

Playing Video Games

Video games are a fun way for me to unwind and connect with friends. These days I mostly enjoy virtual reality games, as I have a VR headset. Some favorites include Blade and Sorcery and VTOL VR. On PC I play more military simulation games such as Squad and Arma III.
